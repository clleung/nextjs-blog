Nice to meet you!

Lisa Leung (she/her) is passionate about learning and finding interdisciplinary solutions with her experience in research (cognitive psychology, usable privacy and security, and intelligent agents), consulting (technology and management), design (user interface, user experience, product-service, designing with new technologies).

In particular, she takes her breadth of experience across disciplines to grow creative, collaborative, and diverse teams.

Lisa is a rising senior in Information Systems at Carnegie Mellon University pursuing an additional degree in Human-Computer Interation and minors in IDeATe Media Design and Societal and Human Impacts of Future Technologies (SHIFT), and will be graduating May 2023.

In her free time, Lisa enjoys reading about organizational behavior (especially HBR books), learning about different cuisines, and seeing the world.
